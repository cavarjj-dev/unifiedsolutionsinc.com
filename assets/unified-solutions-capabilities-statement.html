Unified Solutions Inc. delivers ICF PCC-credentialed executive leadership coaching, group coaching cohorts, team coaching, and professional management development training to federal agencies. We prioritize a unique blend of operational military experience, Fortune 500 consulting depth, and ICF-accredited coaching rigor to support leaders navigating high-stakes organizational leadership and personal transition. Service-Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) headquartered in Plymouth, Minnesota. Available for Micropurchase, Simplified Acquisition, and SDVOSB set-aside engagements.

Core Competencies
1
ICF PCC 1:1 Executive Leadership Coaching
executive coaching · SES coaching · GS-14/15 coaching · senior leader coaching
Holistic, laser-focused 1:1 coaching for senior civilian and military leadership. Bi-weekly 60-minute sessions, defined outcomes, assessment-informed. 3-month minimum; invoiceable to agency. ICF PCC-credentialed. Micropurchase eligible for kick-off engagements.
2
Group Leadership Coaching Cohorts
group coaching · leadership cohort · peer coaching program · leadership facilitation
Structured 4–12 person peer cohort programs for federal leaders. Positive psychology-grounded methodology proven across Cornell, Yale SOM, MIT Sloan, Wharton, and Columbia via ExecOnline. 6–12 week programs. Virtual, in-person, or hybrid delivery.
3
Team Coaching — ICF ACTC-Level Competency
team coaching · leadership team effectiveness · organizational alignment · team development
ICF ACTC-level team coaching addressing trust, alignment, accountability, and communication. Team diagnostic + facilitated full-team sessions + individual touchpoints. 60–90 day standard engagement. Scoped to team size and complexity.
4
Leadership Workshop & Off-Site Facilitation
leadership workshop · executive off-site · leadership training · facilitation
Half-day and full-day evidence-based leadership workshops for agency-wide development events, senior leadership off-sites, and mission-specific programs. SOW-based with pre-event design, facilitation, and post-event summary deliverables.
5
Assessment-Informed Leadership Development
leadership assessment · 360 feedback · personality assessment · development planning
12+ validated instruments: Big Five, HEXACO, TRACOM, SCARF, 360-degree multi-rater. HPTI-certified (Thomas International) and AgileBrain-certified. Assessment + debrief + development plan as discrete contract line item. Micropurchase eligible standalone.
6
Organizational Leadership Development Program Design
leadership development program · workforce development · leadership pipeline · org development
End-to-end design and delivery of multi-cohort agency leadership programs. Needs assessment, curriculum design, facilitation, evaluation. Strategic partnership with Fluence for scale. Proof of delivery: Navy Federal Credit Union VP Cohort, Oct 2025.
