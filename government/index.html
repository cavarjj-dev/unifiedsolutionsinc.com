Procurement Vehicles

How to Engage Unified Solutions Inc.

Available under multiple procurement pathways, including SDVOSB set-aside and sole-source awards. As of October 1, 2025 (FAC 2025-06), updated thresholds expand the range of leadership coaching engagements available without full competition requirements.

Micropurchase — FAR Part 13.2 $15,000
Effective Oct 1, 2025 (FAC 2025-06)
Single coaching sessions, team diagnostic assessments, leadership assessment debriefs, and short-duration facilitation engagements can be scoped within this threshold. Government purchase card (GPC) accepted. No competition required. Fastest path to engagement. Ideal for initial engagements before broader program procurement.
Simplified Acquisition — FAR Part 13 $350,000
Effective Oct 1, 2025 (FAC 2025-06)
Multi-month 1:1 executive coaching engagements, group coaching cohort programs (6–12 weeks), team coaching engagements (60–90 days), and organizational leadership development workshops can be scoped within this threshold. Streamlined FAR Part 13 procedures. SDVOSB set-aside available.

SDVOSB Set-Aside Eligibility: As an SBA-certified SDVOSB, Unified Solutions Inc. is eligible for SDVOSB set-aside and sole-source awards across all federal agencies. The NDAA FY2024 increased the federal SDVOSB spending goal to 5% of all prime and subcontract dollars — representing over $31 billion in annual SDVOSB-targeted procurement. VA Vets First program provides additional sole-source and set-aside opportunities specifically at the Department of Veterans Affairs.
